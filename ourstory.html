OUR STORY

Cameron & Jennifer Webster have spent their careers managing fine dining bars and restaurants across Detroit, Michigan and western North Carolina. They decided to pivot roles after attending numerous formal events and realizing that there was a desperate need for event curators who put hospitality first. With hospitality as our focus, we produce high quality, personalized beverages and interactions for any occasion. We have started an alliance of bartenders that have worked behind reputable bars and have completed BarSmarts, an in-depth bartending and hospitality course insuring staff is well versed in mixology as well as customer service. We will bring appropriate glassware for the beverages you select for the menu.

Food and beverages are first eaten with the eyes, then the touch, then the scent, then the taste— and we don't want you to sacrifice any of that experience on your special day. Our pleasure will be to co-create what you envision, then bring it to life with the utmost care.

We'll take care of the fine details, so you can relax and enjoy.
