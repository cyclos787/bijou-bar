BIJOU BARTENDER ALLIANCE

We look forward to showing you and your guests this kind of unwavering hospitality.

What you can expect:

  • Custom menu with tailored cocktails
  • Real glassware
  • Beautiful garnishes
  • Beer, wine, and non-alcoholic options
